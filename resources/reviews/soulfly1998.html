Self-titled Soulfly I is one of my favourite albums

Soulfly is the debut album by American metal band Soulfly, released on April 21, 1998 through Roadrunner Records.
The record was released in memory of frontman Max Cavalera's deceased stepson and was the first album featuring Cavalera since leaving Sepultura two years prior.
Both CDs have the message In Loving Memory Dana printed across them. The album has been certified gold by the RIAA.
